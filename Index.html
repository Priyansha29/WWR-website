About

At Wrench Wielders Racing, we don’t just build cars—we build legacies. As the official Formula Student team of Symbiosis Institute of Technology, Pune, we are a collective of engineers, dreamers, and doers who turn ideas into race machines. .
‍
Every nut we tighten and every circuit we design carries one belief: real engineering happens on the ground—not just in textbooks. From sleepless nights in the workshop to the roar of engines on track, we live for the rush, the grind, and the glory.

"Designed to perform, Manufactured to win".

Event Details

Formula Bharat is India's premier student motorsport competition, where teams design, build, and race Formula-style cars. Inspired by Formula SAE, it tests engineering skills in vehicle dynamics, powertrain, and cost-efficiency through static and dynamic events. The competition bridges academia and industry, fostering innovation and practical learning for future automotive engineers.

SUPRA SAEINDIA is a national engineering competition where student teams design, build, and race formula-style vehicles. Organized by SAEINDIA, the event promotes innovation, teamwork, and real-world automotive engineering skills, preparing students for careers in motorsports and the automotive industry.
